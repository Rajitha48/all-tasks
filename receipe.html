UMPA

Ingredients:

  • Rava
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Mustard seeds
  • Green chillies
  • Ginger
  • Curry Leaves
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Salt
  • Cashew nuts

Instructions:

  1. Roast rava and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil.
  3. Add mustard seeds.
  4. Add green chillies,ginger,curry leaves.
  5. Add onion.
  6. Add water and salt.
  7. Add rava slowly and mix.
  8. Cover and rest for 2 minutes.
  9. Serve hot.

    An HTML element consists of an opening tag, the content, and a closing tag. For example, a paragraph element includes and with text inside.

    Attributes give additional information about an HTML element. They are written inside the opening tag and help control behavior, such as href in links or src in images.
